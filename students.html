For Students

A Time to Grow

RAX gives you the opportunity to network with previous alumni and begin your search for your career. These are not "guest speakers", but rather interactive mentorship sessions. Like a breakout room, students are encouraged to converse, participate, and ask questions. If you have class/classes during your assigned session, you will be excused from that class (Field Trip) as long as you attend your session. If you sign-up for a session that meets on a date/time you are scheduled to be in an RHS classroom, you might need to relocate and use earbuds for the session. We hope you enjoy these sessions, gain valuable advice, and make some new connections!
