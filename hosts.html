For Hosts

Become a Host

Be a part of cultivating RHS’s Tradition of Excellence! RHS Alumni Externship (RAX) engages alumni by allowing them to virtually host a group of current RHS students and walk them through their career and life journey from RHS to now. Both hosts and current students participate in a symbiotic learning experience with hosts learning about the interests and perspectives of a new generation. Give back to Ridgewood High School by providing insights to the next generation! Sign up now for our 2022 session here!
